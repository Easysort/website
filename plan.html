Easysort

How does Easysort work?

1. Easysort is not an endless funding project that promises an artificially perfect solution while “developing” for years.

2. Easysort is not the easiest way to make money. If you are in it for quick returns, you should probably look elsewhere.

3. Easysort is not going to keep secrets. We strive for transparency by making most of our code and products open source.

4. Easysort is not a bureaucracy mess. We are a small team and move fast.

Easysort does, however, tackle extremely difficult problems in a legacy waste industry using an approach no one else dare to pursue.

Easysort has three main objectives:

  1. Accelerate the transition to a circular economy, with robots sorting all waste perfectly, so only a tiny bit of new material is needed.
  2. Develop intermediate solutions that start making a positive impact from day one.
  3. Be profitable. (Contrary to common belief, this is not always the an objective of some companies.)

How does Easysort make money?

We make money in two ways:

  1. The most straightforward way: We our complete robots to recycling companies.
  2. The less obvious way: We setup our own sorting facility and sell sorted waste to recycling companies.

Ideally 1 is the best way to scale, make money and make a difference. But no one is willing to invest, so we have to do 2 to prove that our solution works. Then maybe 1 will come around again.
