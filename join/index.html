What is Easysort?

1. Easysort is focusing on shipping practical solutions quickly.

2. Easysort is focused on long term, true value over quick cash-outs.

3. Easysort operates transparently. Our strategy, plans and most projects are open source.

4. Easysort is driven by a small, fast-moving team with a close to zero bureaucracy as we can get.

Our Main Objectives

Accelerate the transition to a circular economy with robots sorting all waste perfectly, so only a tiny bit of new material is needed.

Develop intermediate solutions that start making a positive impact from day one.

Be profitable.

How does Easysort make money?

The straightforward way

We sell our complete robots to recycling companies and factories.

The less obvious way

We setup our own sorting facility and sell sorted waste to recycling companies.

Ideally 1 is the best way to scale, make money and make a difference. But no one is willing to invest, so we have to do 2 to prove that our solution works. Then maybe 1 will come around again.
